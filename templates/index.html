About Our AI Model

Understanding how our diabetes prediction system works

Dataset & Methodology

Our AI model is trained on a comprehensive dataset of 1000 patients, sourced from Kaggle (Diabetes Dataset by Aravind PCoder). The data were collected from the Iraqi society, specifically from:

  • Medical City Hospital Laboratory
  • Specializes Center for Endocrinology and Diabetes-Al-Kindy Teaching Hospital

SMOTE-Random Forest Approach

We employ a sophisticated combination of SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique) and Random Forest algorithm to ensure accurate predictions:

  • SMOTE helps balance the dataset by generating synthetic samples
  • Random Forest provides robust classification through ensemble learning
